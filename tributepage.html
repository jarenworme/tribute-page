Pelé - one of the greatest players football has ever seen

Pelé was a Brazilian football player who is credited as shaping the beautiful game to its modern day grandeur.

History

  • Full Name: Edson Arantes do Nascimento
  • Born: October 23, 1940
  • Pelé made his international debut at age 16 in Sweden at the world Cup
  • He retired after leading a US team, Cosmos, to the league championship in 1977
  • Died at the age of 82 in 2021

Achievements

  • 1958: World Cup #1
  • 1962: World Cup #2
  • 1970: World Cup #3
  • 1978: International Peace Award
  • 1980: L’Equipe's Athlete of the Century
  • In addition, Pelé won 6 Brazilian Championships, 2 Copa Libertadores and 2 Intercontinental Cups, just to name a few
